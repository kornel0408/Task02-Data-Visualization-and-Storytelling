# Task 2: Data Visualization and Storytelling

## Internship Details
**Program:** Elevate Labs Data Analyst Internship

**Task:** Data Visualization and Storytelling

## Objective
The objective of this task was to create meaningful visualizations and communicate business insights through interactive dashboards. The analysis focuses on identifying sales trends, customer behavior, product performance, city-wise performance, and conversion metrics using e-commerce sales data.

## Dataset
E-commerce Sales Dataset (Flipkart)

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Excel

## Dashboard Features

### KPI Analysis
- Total Revenue
- Total Customers
- Total Orders
- Total Quantity Sold

### Product Analysis
- Revenue by Category
- Top 5 Products by Revenue
- Product Ratings Analysis
- Quantity Sold by Product

### Customer Analysis
- Revenue Contribution by Gender
- Quantity Purchased by Gender
- Top Customers Analysis

### City Analysis
- Revenue by City
- Quantity Sold by City
- Customer and Order Distribution

### Monthly Trends
- Monthly Revenue Trend
- Peak and Low Sales Period Identification

### Funnel Analysis
- View to Cart Conversion Rate
- Cart to Purchase Conversion Rate
- Overall Conversion Rate

### Business Insights
- Category Performance Analysis
- Top Product Identification
- Customer Behavior Insights
- City-Level Performance Analysis
- Sales Trend Analysis

## Key Findings
- Electronics generated the highest revenue, contributing approximately $4.9M+ in sales.
- July recorded the highest monthly revenue at $1.14M.
- The top 5 products contributed over $300K in total sales.
- North Michael was the highest-performing city by revenue.
- More than 20K orders and 60K units were analyzed.
- The overall conversion rate was 7.15%, indicating opportunities for conversion optimization.

## Outcome
The dashboard successfully transformed raw e-commerce data into actionable business insights through interactive visualizations and storytelling techniques. The analysis helps stakeholders understand customer behavior, product performance, sales trends, and opportunities for business growth.
